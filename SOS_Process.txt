Customer enter cafe/restaurant -> Customer sit on the chair and scan the provided QR code to access the web menu -> Customer see the menu and choose their order -> Customer complete order after checkout -> Customer will be given order number (ex. R-001, D-001) (R is code for Ramen menus, D for drink menus) -> With the given order number, customer will head to the self-service payment desk -> Customer click their order number on the desk's monitor and confirm their order -> The system on the monitor will forward it to the EDC beside the monitor for the customer to pay -> Customer pay with debit/credit card (cashless transaction) -> Customer will get a confirmation notification on the website 'Order Confirmed' -> Customer can track their order status through the website (Waiting payment/In progress/Your order is ready) -> When order is ready, customer can take the order by themselves through the desk.
